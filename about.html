    • Who we are?

    Codigo Plataforma is one of the biggest clubs of Silicon Institute of Technology, Sambalpur that aims to provide a gateway for the people in our institute with an interest in coding to join a community of highly talented coders. Learn new cutting-edge technologies and get benefited from the club's support system.

    We primarily conduct lectures, events, talks, seminars, workshops and even host coding competitions and hackathons. There is something for everyone. Whether you want to learn, participate in hackathons and win big prizes, prepare for your dream company or make successful projects. It is the one-stop destination for all your coding needs!
