    New Point System

    7th MAY, 2021

    This is to inform you about the codigo plataforma's point system. The point system includes the following criterias to awards you with individual points :-

    1. Attendence : 10 points
    2. Assignment : 20 points
    3. Contest : 50 points
    4. For making the class interactive (attentive in live session) : 10 points
    5. Additional challenges : This will be awarded by Student In-charge out of 10.

    This point will be evaluated regularly and the winners will be decide triannually.
